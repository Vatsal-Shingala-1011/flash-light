Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Flashlight Torch Pro ("the App") is developed by quickapps studio. This privacy policy explains how the App handles user information.

Summary: Flashlight Torch Pro does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data or user information. The App operates entirely offline.

Information Collection

The App does not collect any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Name, email address, or contact details
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Browsing history or search queries
  • Photos, media, or files

Permissions

The App requires only the Camera permission to control the device flashlight (torch) via the Camera2 API. This permission is used solely to turn the flashlight on and off. No images or videos are captured, stored, or transmitted.

The App does not require Internet permission. It operates completely offline with no network access.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks. There are no ads in the App.

Data Storage

The App may store minimal preferences (such as language selection) locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Data Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of data breach or unauthorized access to user information.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
