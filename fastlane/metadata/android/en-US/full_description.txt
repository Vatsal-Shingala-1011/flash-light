Flashlight Torch – LED Light is a fast, lightweight flashlight and torch app for your Android phone. Turn your phone into a bright LED flashlight instantly with just one tap.

⚡ Key Features:

🔦 Instant Flashlight – Turn the LED torch on and off with a single tap. No delay, no hassle.

🪢 Pull Rope Switch – Drag the lightbulb like a pull-cord switch to toggle the flashlight with satisfying spring animations. A fun and unique way to control your torch!

📳 Shake to Toggle – Just shake your phone to turn the flashlight on or off. Perfect for quick access in the dark.

💡 Interactive Tutorial – A guided spotlight tutorial helps first-time users discover all the features.

🎨 Dynamic Interface – The background color, light bulb icon, and status bar change dynamically based on the flashlight status for beautiful visual feedback.

🌍 Multi-Language Support – Available in 80+ languages for users worldwide.

🔒 Privacy Focused:
• No internet permission required – works 100% offline
• No ads, no tracking, no analytics
• No personal data collection
• Completely open source

⚙️ Technical Details:
• Uses Camera2 API for reliable flashlight control
• Spring physics animations for a premium feel
• Lightweight app with minimal battery usage
• Supports Android 7.0 and above
